How to Obtain Your Links

1. Visit the Veracross Page
Go to the following link to access your calendar subscriptions:
https://portals.veracross.com/esj/student/calendar/subscribe/mine
2. Copy the Required Links
On the subscription page, you will see two specific buttons.
Right-click on each button and select "Copy Link Address" to obtain the necessary URLs.
